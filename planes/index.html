Planes and Design

Since we were given the location of the library, we decided to create something along the lines of a book. The book flaps were created by large carboard sheets folded in so that the inside is actually hollow. It was then painted white, with the sides a light gray, and the back as black. Then, we painted a tree to symbolize a "tree of knowledge" and hung books around it with fishing wire. To finish it up, I myself painted a dragon on the right side, and cut+glued together a dragon head that protruded out. Installion was tricky but we got everything attached and stable within a few days of work.
