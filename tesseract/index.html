Lines and Space- Constructing a Tesseract

For this project, I worked with Aman, and we came up with the idea of a tesseract- a cube inside a cube as it would demonstrate both line and space, and create a cool effect.
To make this, we used tightly rolled paper:
Bottom: double sticks taped together
Sides: Triple tight sticks taped together
Top: Same as bottom but with wires inside for support
The center cube was made of rolled paper and was held up by transparent strings
